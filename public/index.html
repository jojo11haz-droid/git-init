Before you start

How Between uses your check-ins

  • Your check-ins are shared with your therapist as short summaries — not always the raw recording.
  • If you turn on AI summaries below, an AI model analyzes what you send to write the summary and to find patterns over time (mood, themes, timing). This counts as "profiling" of your health information.
  • You can turn this off and still send check-ins — they'll go to your therapist as you typed or recorded them, without AI analysis.
  • You can see everything the app has inferred about your patterns at any time, and ask for it to be deleted.

Do my patients pay anything?

No. Patients never see a bill — Between is licensed to the clinician or practice, and patients are invited in at no cost to them.

What happens after my trial?

No credit card is required to start. You'll get a reminder before your trial ends, and nothing is charged unless you choose a plan.

Is this a replacement for my EHR?

No — Between focuses specifically on between-session check-ins. It's designed to run alongside whatever practice management or notes software you already use.

Is my patients' data stored in Canada?

Yes. Check-in data and AI processing are kept within Canadian data residency, consistent with Quebec's Law 25 and other provincial privacy requirements.
